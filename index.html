Welcome to Kisan College

Established in 1957, Kisan College has been a pioneer in providing quality education across various disciplines. Our institution is committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for global challenges.

With a sprawling 50-acre campus, state-class infrastructure, and a team of highly qualified faculty members, we offer an environment that fosters academic excellence, research, and holistic development.
